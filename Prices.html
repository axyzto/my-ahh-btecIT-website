Prices of the TUFF Festival.

Tuff Festival is commemorating a remarkable milestone—100 years of music, memories, and community spirit. What began as a humble local event has blossomed into a beloved tradition that attracts hundreds of thousands of attendees each year. This year's festival presents a unique opportunity to reflect on our rich history while simultaneously crafting new memories for future generations.

Emphasizing family-friendly rock, the music at Tuff Festival promises live performances that are fun, energetic, and suitable for all ages. Attendees can look forward to a diverse lineup of bands that embody the essence of rock, engaging both children and adults alike. With catchy tunes and uplifting rhythms, our music is designed to bring families together, fostering a spirit of celebration and joy.

In addition to the superb musical experience, the festival features an array of food stalls, exciting games, and activities for visitors of all ages. From delicious culinary delights to hands-on creative workshops, there’s something for everyone in our warm and inviting atmosphere. By offering various price packages, including the Family Fun Package, Rockstar Experience, and Community Celebration Pass, Tuff Festival ensures that every attendee can enjoy our blend of entertainment, history, and community, making it a celebration that is truly special.
