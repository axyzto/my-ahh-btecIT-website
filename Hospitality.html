Hospitality at the TUFF Festival.

At Tuff Festival, we strive to make your experience unforgettable by offering exceptional VIP hospitality options designed to elevate your festival enjoyment. As we celebrate our 100th anniversary, our commitment to excellence is stronger than ever, ensuring that every attendee can participate in a truly memorable celebration. Our VIP packages provide exclusive access to premium amenities and services that create a delightful atmosphere for friends, families, and fellow music lovers.

Our VIP selection includes luxurious dining experiences with sit-down catering throughout the festival, allowing you to savor delicious meals while enjoying the music. From breakfast to snacks, our gourmet options cater to all tastes, enhancing your overall experience. Additionally, our VIP accommodation features comfortable glamping pods complete with personal catering and essential facilities, ensuring a cozy retreat after exhilarating days of entertainment.

Furthermore, VIP attendees enjoy exclusive access to the golden circle area at the stage, granting the best views of live performances. Beyond these luxurious offerings, all festival-goers will find essential amenities, including water stations and spacious restroom facilities. Tuff Festival is committed to creating a welcoming environment for everyone, ensuring that this milestone celebration is filled with music, community, and unforgettable memories.

VIP Dining
 Experience culinary excellence with our VIP Dining option, featuring sit-down catering throughout the festival. Enjoy a variety of curated meals, including gourmet breakfasts to start your day, sumptuous lunches, delightful snacks, and elegant dinners. Each meal showcases local and seasonal produce, prepared by top chefs to provide an unforgettable dining experience for our VIP guests. Dietary preferences and restrictions are accommodated to ensure everyone can indulge in the festivities. Pricing is offered on a fixed per-meal basis, with the option to purchase a comprehensive package that covers all meals during the festival—providing convenience and a seamless experience.
VIP Accomodation
Revel in the comfort of our VIP Accommodation, featuring luxurious glamping pods situated in a prime location within the festival grounds. Each pod is designed for relaxation and privacy, offering personal catering, quality bedding, and private toilet facilities to elevate your festival experience. Guests can unwind after a day of excitement in a peaceful retreat, complete with décor that enhances the ambiance of your exclusive stay. Guests will pay a one-time fee to secure their luxurious glamping pod for the entire duration of the festival, ensuring a private and comfortable environment. Early booking is highly recommended due to limited availability.
VIP Stage Location
Gain exclusive access to our VIP Stage Location, which offers a premium viewing area in front of the stage, also known as the golden circle. This prime spot allows for an unrivaled festival experience—enjoying live performances up close and personal. The golden circle is designed to provide comfort, allowing guests to enjoy the show without the crowds, making it a perfect choice for the ultimate music lover. The VIP Stage Location is available for purchase as a premium ticket option. Advance booking is required to secure your place in this coveted area, as spaces are limited and fill up quickly.
Water Stations
Stay refreshed and hydrated with our strategically placed water stations throughout the festival grounds. Our commitment to sustainability ensures that these stations provide free access to clean drinking water for all attendees, reducing plastic use and promoting eco-friendly practices. The water stations are easily identifiable and staffed for convenience, ensuring that hydration is just a few steps away. Access to water stations is completely free of charge, as part of our commitment to sustainability and guest comfort.
