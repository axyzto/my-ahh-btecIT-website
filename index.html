Rocking your world since 20-20-TUFF.

Tuff Festival is celebrating a huge milestone — 100 years of music, memories and community spirit. First held a century ago, the festival has grown from a small local event into a tradition that brings hundreds of thousands of people together each year. This year's festival is a chance to look back at our amazing history while creating new memories for the next generation.

The music at Tuff Festival is all about family-friendly rock, with live performances designed to be fun, energetic and safe for everyone. The stage will host a variety of bands who keep the spirit of rock alive in a way that both kids and adults can enjoy. From catchy tunes to uplifting rhythms, the music is made to bring families together in celebration.

Alongside the music, the festival also offers food stalls, fun games and activities for visitors of all ages. Families can enjoy everything from tasty treats to creative workshops, all in a welcoming and festive atmosphere. With our mix of entertainment, history and community, Tuff Festival is more than just a concert. It's a tradition worth celebrating.
